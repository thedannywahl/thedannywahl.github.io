Zebra 2.0+ for Moodle 2

10 May 2011 on CSS, Moodle, Theme

I’ve finally finished porting Zebra to Moodle 2.0, with a big thank you to Mary Evans for the CSS for the custom menu.  The major new feature of this is a brand new page layout that I’ve written – one that isn’t Moodle specific (despite what others may say…) and can be ported to any website.  This page layout uses the CSS3 @media declaration to provide a 1, 2, or 3 column flowing layout with a 2-1-3 column order (meaning your main content always comes first) but I’ll write more about that later.

Anyways, download it here, play with it, then tell me what’s broken here.
